Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza Crust
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • Topping

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 450Â°F (or the temperature specified on your pizza crust package).
  2. Place your pizza crust on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  3. Spread a layer of tomato sauce evenly over the pizza crust, leaving about 1/2 inch of space around the edges for the crust to expand.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the tomato sauce, again leaving some space around the edges.
  5. Add your desired toppings to the pizza. Be sure not to overload the pizza with too many toppings, as this can make it difficult to cook properly.
  6. Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the crust is golden brown
  7. Once the pizza is finished baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
